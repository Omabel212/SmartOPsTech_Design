Delivery

Three ways to learn.

Every programme runs in three delivery formats. We work with you to find the one that fits your team’s operating reality.

01

Public cohort

Individual engineers join a scheduled cohort with peers from other organisations. Excellent for cross-industry perspective and networking. Runs quarterly from a central UK location.

02

On-site team

We deliver the programme at your facility with your engineers, using examples drawn from your processes and data. Most cost-effective for groups of 6 or more.

03

Hybrid & virtual

Live virtual sessions with a dedicated facilitator, paired with hands-on practical labs and asynchronous coursework. Designed for teams distributed across sites or geographies.
